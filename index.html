FAQ

Is Mandalar a Shopify app?

Yes — and your forms can also be used on non‑Shopify sites via a share link or embed.

Can I use my form name in the URL?

Yes. We generate a readable slug like book-your-seat-for-abc-event.

Can I embed it anywhere?

Yes. Start with iframe embed (works everywhere). A JS embed option can come next.

Who owns the data?

Your client (the merchant) owns the submissions. Mandalar stores them to provide the service.

Do you support event registrations?

Absolutely — registrations are a core use case for Mandalar.

Where do I build the forms?

In the app at app.mandalar.app.
