Overview

Mandalar provides form building and submission storage for merchants and organisations. We process submissions on behalf of our customers.

Data we collect

  • Account details for app users (e.g. shop domain, installation identifiers).
  • Form submissions collected via Mandalar forms (as configured by the customer).
  • Basic technical data (e.g. IP hash, user agent) for fraud/spam prevention.

How we use data

  • To provide the Mandalar service and customer support.
  • To secure and maintain the platform, including preventing abuse.
  • To comply with legal obligations (including Shopify GDPR webhooks where applicable).

Retention

Customers can delete forms and submissions. When an app is uninstalled, we delete associated customer data within a reasonable period.

Your rights

If you are an end-user submitting a form, please contact the organisation/merchant that collected your data. They control the purpose and use.
